How it works?

  • 01 Set requirements Make your profile and tell us what you want. Customise your competencies and set up their difficulty and importance
  • 02 Convert into mind maps Leverage our deep learning algorithms and convert your set competencies into a neutral network. Let our AI pick the questions set based on adaptive testing
  • 03 See the magic Our bots will assess the students and find the right fit for your role with over 90 per cent accuracy.
